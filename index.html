What is Der Bullen?

Der Bullen is German for The Bulls, one of the greatest sports dynasties of all time! And, like The Bulls, we want to produce the same pedigree in the Fantasy Football arena, week in & week out. We've been members of the IDL since it's inception in 2015, and we aren't going anywhere.

Scott Lesser

Der Bullen GM

When the Invitational Dynasty League added Der Bullen in their 2015 expansion, Scott Lesser was the 4th or 5th manager Der Bullen called. After 10+ seasons without a fantasy championship, he wasn't the obvious choice. Honestly, Der Bullen didn't really want him. But, after everyone else said no, they pulled the trigger.

So far, he has not impressed. He did manage to improve the team from 10th in the league, to 6th. But, it looks like they will be losing their best player to a 6-week suspension to start the season. Can they still scrape together enough wins to get to the playoffs?
